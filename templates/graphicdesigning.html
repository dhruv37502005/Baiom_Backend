Graphic Designing

  • Understand the principles of graphic design, including color theory, typography, and layout
  • Develop your skills in using graphic design software, including Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign
  • Learn to create designs for various media, including print and digital
  • Understand how to communicate visually and create effective designs
  • Build your knowledge of design history and contemporary design trends
  • Develop your portfolio to showcase your work

  • Textbook: We will use a coursebook designed specifically for our Graphic Designing course.
