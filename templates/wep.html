Importance and Oppurtunities

01

Importance of Entrepreneurship Programs for Women:

Provide a platform for women to explore and pursue their passions. Equip women with essential skills and knowledge for navigating the business world. Foster financial independence, allowing women to control their futures autonomously.

02

Future Outlook for Women in Entrepreneurship Programs:

Continued growth in women-owned businesses, contributing significantly to the economy. Increased opportunities and support for women entrepreneurs. Focus on digitalization as a facilitator for women-owned start-ups.

03

Opportunities Created by Entrepreneurship Programs:

Building a trustworthy and talented team is key to success. Emphasizing communication, collaboration, and mutual elevation within the team. Acknowledging that teamwork is essential, and setting achievable goals consistently leads to success. Entrepreneurship programs open doors for women to establish their brands and contribute to various industries. Learning and adapting from experiences, building on smaller successes to achieve larger goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. I am not a student of BAOIAM. Can I still refer a friend?
Yes, If you are aware of BAOIAM and believe in what we are doing, then feel free to refer your friend.
Q2. How can I track the status of my referrals?
You will be informed by email once BAOIAM receives your referral details.
Q3. How will my friends be informed that I referred them?
Your friends will receive an email confirmation from BAOIAM informing them that they have been referred by you. They will be asked to confirm.
Q4. Will I get a referral reward if a friend/connection already exists in the BAOIAM database?
No, there is no reward if your friend is connected with BAOIAM or its infromation exists in BAOIAM database
Q5. Who all can I refer to?
Anyone who meets the eligibility criteria for our courses can be referred. They should be between 18 - 28 years of age. They should know the basics of the English language, have a laptop, and a good internet connection.
